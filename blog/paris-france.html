Paris, France

I was visiting my best friend Cleonie in Paris and she planned the most scenic route through Paris during sunset. A man in a bright green and pink suit precariously stands on a suitcase while dancing to ‘Everybody Wants to Rule the World’, leading a growing crowd of people through a dance. I did not feel coordinated enough to join but did anyways. Despite the cold winds of December, there was so much warmth in a group of strangers dancing together because a guy on a suitcase with a speaker said to.
