Delft, Netherlands

I had landed in the evening for a conference in Delft and was waiting to have dinner with my friend Hiya who had flown in from India. While I strolled the warmly lit streets, I saw a pub closed for the day and I snuck my camera through the back window to capture this shot. Even the loudest of pubs need a moment of peace.
